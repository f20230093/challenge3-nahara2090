# Challenge 3 – Nahara 2090: The Last Oasis

## Overview
This repository contains my submission materials for Challenge 3: AI Poster Designer.

The project explores a fictional futuristic desert world titled **Nahara 2090: The Last Oasis**. The goal was to use generative AI as a creative ideation tool, explore multiple poster directions, and refine one final concept into a polished poster.

## Concept
The poster imagines a cinematic sci-fi desert city that feels atmospheric, elegant, and mysterious. The visual direction combines warm desert tones, futuristic architecture, vast landscapes, and a premium editorial mood.

## Initial Poster Directions
Three initial visual directions were explored:

1. **Cinematic Futuristic Desert City**
2. **Editorial Desert Traveler**
3. **Minimal Symbolic Desert Poster**

After comparison, the cinematic city direction was selected as the final concept because it had the strongest composition, the clearest focal point, and the most immersive poster quality.

## Refinement Process
The selected AI generated concept was refined manually in Canva. The refinement focused on:
- typography hierarchy
- title and subtitle placement
- tagline placement
- visual balance
- preserving the generated image as the main focal point

## Final Rationale
My poster is based on a fictional futuristic desert world called **Nahara 2090: The Last Oasis**. The aim was to create a cinematic visual identity for a place that feels mysterious, elegant, and advanced, while still rooted in the atmosphere of sand, light, and isolation.

I explored three poster directions using generative AI:
- a cinematic futuristic desert city
- an editorial desert traveler concept
- a minimal symbolic desert composition

I selected the cinematic city direction because it had the strongest visual impact, the clearest composition, and the most poster like presence.

The final poster was refined manually in Canva by adjusting composition, typography, hierarchy, and placement so that the image remained the focal point while the text supported the concept clearly.
